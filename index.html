About

Biography

I am an Associate Professor in Marketing at the University of Birmingham and Director of the Ph.D. Programme. As an active researcher in marketing strategy, I conduct work in the branding, technology, and services marketing areas, with a focus on understanding how value is created for different stakeholders during technology and service interactions.

My research further explores the role of branding in emerging technology contexts — including the platform economy, cryptocurrencies, and NFTs. I have published in leading academic outlets including the Journal of Business Research, Tourism Management, British Journal of Management, European Journal of Marketing, Psychology & Marketing, Annals of Tourism Research, and the European Management Review.

My teaching interests span Marketing Research, Branding, and Services Marketing across undergraduate, MBA, and Ph.D./DBA levels. I have presented at EMAC, AMS, and AMA, and serve as Associate Editor for three journals. I am also a contributor to practitioner and public outlets including The Conversation, Business Leader, and Capital.gr.
